Mohsen Ghafoorian


I am a Sr. Staff ML researcher, technical team lead and manager at Qualcomm working on augmented/virtual reality applictions. Prior to this I was a senior deep leaning R&D engineer, technical team-lead and line-manager at TomTom, working on automated generation of high-definition maps for autonomous driving. I recieved my Ph.D in machine learning from Radboud University, the Netherlands, where I worked on development of machine learning algorithms for detection and characterization of neuro-degenerative diseases (Oct. 2013 - June 2017). My Ph.D. project was in collaboration between the Machine Learning and the Diagnostic Image Analysis Groups and was under the supervision of Dr. Bram Platel, Prof. Elena Marchiori, Prof. Nico Karssemeijer and Prof. Tom Heskes.
